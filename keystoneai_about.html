Our Mission

Keystone AI is pioneering AI-powered intelligence systems tailored for Pakistan’s public sector. Our mission is to bring modern data-driven governance to life through scalable, predictive platforms.

The Problem

Pakistan suffers major losses from disasters like floods due to the lack of real-time data integration and predictive intelligence. The 2022 Swat floods alone caused widespread devastation.

Our Solution: KIGAL

The Khyber Intelligence and Governance Analytics Lab (KIGAL) is our flagship initiative. It uses AI to fuse satellite, drone, and ground-level data into early-warning systems and governance dashboards—starting with flood intelligence and expanding into nationwide risk management and decision support.
